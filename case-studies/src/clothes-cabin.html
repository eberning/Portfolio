Clothes Cabin

Project

Non-profit organization responsive webiste redesign.

Summary

I and my teammates choose to redesign the Clothes Cabin website in order to prove the authenticity of the non-profit organization.

Role

UX/UI Designer & Reseracher

Tools

Adobe XD
Figma
Miro

How it was? The problem.

  • User are unable to find what they need because of the poor website navigation
  • The quality of the website has led to a mistrust in the organization’s legitimacy
  • Users are unable to find hours of operation and location

Our solution!

Our goal was to redesign the navigation system, display organization mission, highlight location and hours of operations for a smooth user experince.

User-persona

Based on our analysis and observations we created fictional perosna. A potentional user of Clothes Cabin website with it’s pain points, goals and motivations.

We want to support people in need and our community, but very often we don’t know if the organizations that we look at are legitimate.
Brands
Bio

Sandra and Tom are a married couple wih 5 grandchildren, who still work hard despite their age. They grew up in loving homes and received good educations. They spend most of their free time either reading books or volunteering at non-profit organizations. They want to sell their business one day and travel to 3rd world countries to help children in poverty.

Goals & Interests
  • Wants to help low income families
  • Needs to spend more time with her grandkids
  • Wants to travel to third world countries
  • Wants to support their local community
  • Believes it’s important to donate to charities every month

We then identified our User insight statement

Users want to have confidence & trust in an organization that their donations are being used to further the cause they support.

Our User insight lead us to Clothes Cabin’s Mission
